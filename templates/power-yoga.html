Strength in Motion

Power Yoga is a fitness-based vinyasa practice. It takes the athleticism of Ashtanga, including lots of vinyasas, but gives the instructor the freedom to teach any poses in any order, making every class a unique challenge.

By focusing on flow and building internal heat, this practice acts as a moving meditation that challenges your physical limits while helping you find a deep sense of calm within the intensity.

"Power is not just about muscle; it's about the steady mind that moves through the fire."

Empowering Benefits

Cardiovascular Health

Boosts metabolism and improves heart health through sustained, rhythmic movement and increased heart rate.

Lean Muscle & Endurance

Builds total-body strength and lean muscle mass while significantly increasing your physical endurance.

Focus & Resilience

Sharpens mental focus and builds emotional resilience, leaving you energized and ready to take on any challenge.

Core Pillars

Internal Heat

Continuous movement creates "Tapas" (heat) to detoxify the body and improve flexibility.

Vinyasa Flow

Breath-synchronized movement that creates a seamless, energetic transition between poses.

Total Toning

Engages every muscle group, from the core to the extremities, for a full-body workout.

Mental Grit

Holding challenging poses longer to build psychological strength and patience.
