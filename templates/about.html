Our Story

Founded with a vision to transform lives through integrated wellness, Wellnation360 combines ancient wisdom with modern science to create personalized health solutions. Our journey began with a simple belief - that true wellness comes from balancing mind, body and spirit.

Our Vision

Our vision is to empower individuals and communities throughout India by fostering holistic wellness through personalized health and nutrition guidance. We strive to inspire a balanced lifestyle that incorporates physical, emotional, and nutritional well-being, leading to a healthier, happier, and more fulfilled life. We envision a future for India where optimal health is not just a goal, but a sustainable reality for everyone To become India’s leading integrative wellness hub—uniting nutrition, yoga, and natural healing—to empower every individual toward a healthier, balanced life in alignment with the vision of ‘One Earth, One Health’.

Our Mission

Our mission is to make holistic health achievable and attainable across age groups. We go by the objective “Prevention is better than cure”. We intend to contribute significantly towards the salient objective of nation building via ensuring that India is able to genuinely benefit from its demographic dividend •To offer a comprehensive wellness ecosystem rooted in India’s ancient wisdom and modern science—encompassing personalized nutrition, yoga, meditation, and alternative therapies like acupuncture and healing massage. •To promote physical, emotional, and spiritual balance across all age groups through guided, inclusive, and culturally resonant wellness programs. •To contribute to the Fit India vision and global health harmony by making holistic well-being accessible, aspirational, and sustainable for all.

Yogakshema

Derived from ancient Indian wisdom, Yogakshema represents holistic wellness and personal liberation. Through therapeutic yoga practices, we aim to nurture the mind, body, and spirit in harmony.

Our yoga therapies are designed to address specific health concerns while promoting overall wellbeing. Each session combines:

  • Asanas (postures) tailored to individual needs
  • Pranayama (breath control techniques)
  • Meditation and relaxation practices
  • Lifestyle recommendations
