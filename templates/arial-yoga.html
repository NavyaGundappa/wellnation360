Why Practice in the Air?

Aerial Yoga uses a soft fabric hammock to support your body weight. This unique tool allows you to achieve deep stretches and complex inversions that might be difficult on the floor.

By offloading weight from your joints, you can focus on core engagement and spinal alignment with zero compression, leaving you feeling taller and lighter.

Core Benefits

Spinal Health

Inversions allow the spine to decompress, relieving pressure between the vertebrae and reducing back pain.

Deeper Stretches

The hammock acts as a partner, helping you sink deeper into poses safely while enhancing overall flexibility.

Joint Relief

Unlike floor yoga, Aerial reduces the impact on knees and wrists, making it accessible for those with joint sensitivities.

Prepare for Flight

To ensure a safe and comfortable experience, please keep the following in mind for your first class.

  • Wear fitted clothing (to avoid getting caught in the fabric)
  • Avoid jewelry or zippers that could snag the hammocks
  • Long sleeves/leggings are recommended for comfort
  • Avoid a heavy meal 2 hours before the session
