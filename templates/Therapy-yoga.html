Movement as Medicine

Therapy Yoga isn't about perfect poses; it's about functional healing. We combine traditional yoga wisdom with modern therapeutic techniques to address the root causes of discomfort.

Whether you are recovering from a sports injury, managing chronic back pain, or seeking relief from emotional stress, each session is adapted to your body's specific needs and limitations.

"We meet you where you are today, to help you get where you want to be tomorrow."

Healing Benefits

Injury Recovery

Supports faster rehabilitation and provides significant pain relief by strengthening supporting muscles around injuries.

Joint Mobility

Increases range of motion and improves circulation, reducing stiffness and promoting long-term joint health.

Emotional Release

Provides a safe space for somatic emotional release, aiding in mental clarity and stress reduction.
