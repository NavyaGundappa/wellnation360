Alignment and Accessibility

Props are not a sign of weakness; they are tools of intelligence. They allow the body to experience the full benefit of an asana without compromising breath or skeletal alignment.

Whether you are looking to bridge a gap in flexibility, hold a restorative pose longer, or target specific muscle groups with pinpoint accuracy, practicing with props transforms how you understand your body's mechanics.

"Props bring the floor closer to you, extending your reach and expanding your possibilities."

Supported Benefits

Enhances Precision

Improves posture precision and depth by allowing you to focus on structural alignment rather than straining to reach a shape.

Reduces Risk of Injury

Prevents overstretching and joint compression by providing a safe framework for muscles to open up gradually.

Therapeutic & Accessible

Ideal for beginners learning the fundamentals, or for those recovering from injuries needing a therapeutic approach.

Your Toolkit for Support

Yoga Blocks

Bring the floor up to you, providing height and stability in standing poses and deep stretches.

Yoga Straps

Safely extend your reach, allowing you to open the shoulders and stretch the hamstrings without tension.

Bolsters

Firm, supportive pillows that allow the body to deeply relax in restorative postures for longer periods.

Blankets

Provide gentle cushioning for sensitive joints like knees and wrists, or add subtle height for seated poses.
